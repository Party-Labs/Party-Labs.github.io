## Summary
Party Labs was founded in 2019 as an entity focused on research and understanding in cybersecurity. We are committed to identifying, analyzing and addressing vulnerabilities and threats in an evolving digital environment. With a dedication to excellence in research and collaborative work, we create a platform for individuals who are passionate about maintaining the integrity of cybersecurity.

## Organization Description
Party Labs is a community that aims to address complex challenges in cybersecurity. Founded in 2019, we engage in in-depth research and analysis to address the latest threats in the digital world. We encourage cross-disciplinary collaboration, with a focus on developing adaptive and effective solutions. With a vision to create a safer digital ecosystem, we combine expertise and dedication to achieve extraordinary results

## Vision
To be an innovation hub that guides the development of trusted and effective cybersecurity solutions, driving positive changes in the global digital threat map.

## Mission
1. Conduct in-depth research and analysis regarding the latest vulnerabilities in digital technology, both at the hardware and software level.
2. Develop a proactive and adaptive approach in addressing increasingly sophisticated cyberthreats.
3. Encouraging collaboration between the industrial sector, academia, and the government in mitigating cyber risks.
4. Contribute actively to the global security community through participation in events, conferences and joint research initiatives.
